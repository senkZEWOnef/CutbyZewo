❓ Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Click “Sign Up” in the top right corner. To reset your password, log out and click “Forgot Password?” on the login screen.

A Job is a cut plan/client project. Click “➕ Create Job” on the home page, fill out the client name, parts, panels and deadlines. Once saved, you can always edit the job later.

Cut sheets are automatically created based on your part dimensions and material/thickness. They are saved in static/sheets/<job_id> and can also be exported to PDF inside a Job.

You can add accessories (drawer slides, hinges, laminate, etc) and your own prices. The system totals up the accessories, install fees and commission to calculate a final estimate.

Use Stocks to manage your shop inventory (e.g., sheets of plywood, hardware items). You can increment/decrement quantities as you use materials on jobs.

When you add uploads to a Job, they appear in the Job Gallery and are stored in static/uploads/<job_id>. They do not affect cut layout but are for your internal reference.
